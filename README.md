## 📌 Overview

NiyamSetu AI is a domain-specific regulatory intelligence platform designed to automate compliance orchestration within banking ecosystems.

The platform transforms unstructured financial regulations, circulars, and compliance advisories into actionable workflows using:

* 🧠 Domain-adapted NLP
* 🤖 Multi-agent AI systems
* 🔎 Semantic regulatory retrieval
* 📊 Risk-aware compliance analytics
* 🕸️ Compliance knowledge graphs
* ✅ Autonomous validation pipelines

Unlike traditional compliance dashboards, NiyamSetu AI acts as an intelligent compliance operating system capable of understanding, mapping, validating, and monitoring regulatory obligations in real time.

---

# 🚨 Problem Statement

Modern banks face:

* Constantly evolving regulations
* Manual compliance interpretation
* Fragmented implementation workflows
* Delayed regulatory response
* Audit complexity
* High operational risk

Current systems are largely:

* Reactive
* Rule-based
* Human-dependent
* Non-explainable

NiyamSetu AI addresses these limitations through autonomous regulatory intelligence.
